Sidi Bou Said

Welcome to the Sidi Bou Said page!

Sidi Bou Said is a charming coastal village located just outside of Tunis, known for its stunning blue and white architecture.

The village is perched on a cliff overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, offering breathtaking views and a picturesque setting.
